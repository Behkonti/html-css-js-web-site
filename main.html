Braking

Do not brake sharply in front of heavy-duty cars: they may not have time to stop behind you.

A human shield.

Some drivers use a human shield - they make a turn or U-turn under the cover of a tram, bus, truck.

Seat belts.

The devices should not press the driver hard against the seat.

Correct landing.

The seat is installed in such a way that the driver's legs are slightly bent at the knee with the clutch pedal fully depressed.

Adjustment of mirrors.

When setting up the interior mirror, make sure that the entire rear window is displayed in it.

Setting up the steering column.

The position of the steering wheel is adjusted using a special lock.
