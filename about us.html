About us

The interactive driving school "CREDO" is a modern technology for training drivers of category "B"! Complex Traffic Rules have become simple, understandable and accessible for a cadet of ANY AGE Driving School. You can see for yourself the quality of training by attending classes as a spectator. As a rule, people like an accessible and interesting program, a young, creative teacher, teaching methods, and they continue to attend classes, but already as future drivers. Instructors, in practical driving, teach not only "how to drive", but also "why and how it works". They help to adapt to urban driving conditions, provide psychological assistance in difficult road situations.
